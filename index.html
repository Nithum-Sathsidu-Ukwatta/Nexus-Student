Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 13, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 13, 2026

Nexus Learning Assistant ("the Application", "the App", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle, collect, use, and safeguard your information and data when you use our mobile application.

The Application is designed as an AI-powered learning assistant to help students (particularly those preparing for GCE O/L and A/L examinations) generate study timetables, create flashcards, complete MCQs, utilize Pomodoro focus timers, and access an interactive AI tutor.

We value your privacy and operate with maximum transparency. We do not require user account registration, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

1. Information Collection and Use

A. Device Permissions Requested

To deliver core educational features, the Application requests the following standard Android system permissions:

  • CAMERA (android.permission.CAMERA):
    Purpose: Allows the App to capture images of study papers, handwritten notes, or textbooks so the AI Tutor can analyze the text and assist you.
    Data Handling: Captured images are processed transiently to send to the Gemini API for analysis. They are NOT stored on our servers or shared with any third parties other than Google's Gemini API processor.
  • INTERNET & NETWORK STATE (android.permission.INTERNET):
    Purpose: Allows the App to securely communicate with the Google Gemini API to generate academic materials, answer questions, and perform connectivity testing.
  • POST NOTIFICATIONS (android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS):
    Purpose: Enables the App to deliver Pomodoro focus timer alerts and session completion reminders on Android 13 devices and above.

B. User-Generated Data

  • Local Databases: Your personalized study timetables, custom flashcards, quiz scores, and settings are stored entirely on your device using a local Room/SQLite database. This data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the App.

2. Third-Party Services & API Processing

To provide generative learning features, the Application utilizes trusted third-party service providers:

  • Google Gemini API:
    Text prompts, academic questions, and scan images you submit to the AI tutor or generators are processed by the Google Gemini API to return educational feedback. This processing is subject to Google’s terms of service and Google's Generative AI privacy policies. Data sent via the API is typically governed by enterprise terms and is secure. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for detailed information.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our own servers, we do not host or hold your personal information.

  • All user study progress, schedules, and custom cards remain exclusively in local device storage.
  • You can wipe all stored data at any time by clearing the App's storage/cache in your Android system settings or by uninstalling the Application.

4. Children's Privacy

The Application is target-designed for GCE Ordinary Level (O/L) and Advanced Level (A/L) students, who may be under the age of 18.

  • Because Nexus Learning Assistant collects no personal information, usernames, emails, phone numbers, or location info, the App complies with international children's privacy regulations, including the United States Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and Google Play’s Families Policy.
  • If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information (such as through a direct support email), we will immediately delete that information from our records.

5. Security

The security of your local data is highly important to us. Although no electronic transmission or local storage mechanism is 100% secure, we follow standard industry practices, utilize Android's secure app sandboxing, and transmit all AI prompts exclusively over encrypted HTTPS connections.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
